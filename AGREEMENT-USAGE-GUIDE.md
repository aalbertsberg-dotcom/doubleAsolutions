# Consulting Agreement - Usage Guide

## 🎉 What You Got

A professional, legally-sound consulting services agreement with:
- ✅ Your hexagon badge logo
- ✅ Liability limitation (protects you from massive lawsuits)
- ✅ Warranty disclaimers (protects from "it didn't work" claims)
- ✅ Termination clause (how to end bad relationships)
- ✅ Scope change process (prevents scope creep)
- ✅ Independent contractor status (tax protection)
- ✅ Payment terms with late fees
- ✅ Intellectual property clauses
- ✅ Confidentiality agreement
- ✅ Insurance mention
- ✅ Dispute resolution process

---

## 🚀 How to Use It

### **Method 1: Fill Out Digitally & Print**

1. **Open the HTML file** in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. **Click on the underlined fields** - they're editable!
   - Date fields
   - Client name/address
   - Services description
   - Rate and payment terms
3. **Fill everything out** right in the browser
4. **Click "Print / Save as PDF"** button (top right)
5. **Choose "Save as PDF"** in the print dialog
6. **Email the PDF** to your client
7. **They print, sign, scan, and return**
8. **You sign your copy**

---

### **Method 2: Use with DocuSign (Recommended)**

1. **Fill out the HTML** in browser (as above)
2. **Save as PDF**
3. **Upload to DocuSign**
4. **Add signature fields** in DocuSign:
   - Your signature line
   - Client signature line
   - Both date fields
5. **Send to client** via DocuSign
6. **Client signs electronically**
7. **Both parties get signed copy automatically**

**DocuSign Free:** 3 envelopes/month - https://www.docusign.com

---

### **Method 3: Print Blank & Fill by Hand**

1. **Open HTML in browser**
2. **Print without filling anything out**
3. **Fill in by hand with pen**
4. **Sign in person**

## 💼 What Each Section Protects

### **Section 1 - Services:**
Be SPECIFIC about what you will and won't do. Vague = scope creep.

**Good:**
```
"Configure Microsoft 365 tenant, migrate 50 mailboxes from on-premise 
Exchange to Exchange Online, provide documentation."
```

**Bad:**
```
"IT consulting and support"
```

---

### **Section 9 - Limitation of Liability (CRITICAL):**
This caps your liability at the fees paid. Without this, you could be sued for millions.

**Example:** Client pays you $5,000 for a migration. Something goes wrong, they lose data. Without this clause, they could sue for $500,000. WITH this clause, maximum liability is $5,000.

---

### **Section 8 - Warranties (CRITICAL):**
The "AS IS" disclaimer protects you from guaranteeing specific results.

**Why this matters:** You can't guarantee "zero downtime" or "100% security." This clause protects you when things don't go perfectly.

**After Signing:**
1. Save signed PDF to Google Drive folder: "Contracts"
2. Name it: "Agreement-[ClientName]-[Date].pdf"
3. Keep for at least 7 years (IRS requirement)

## 💰 Insurance Note

Section 11 mentions you have E&O insurance. If you DON'T have it yet:

**Option 1:** Get E&O Insurance
- Cost: $500-1500/year
- Protects you from malpractice claims
- Recommended if you're doing serious infrastructure work

**Option 2:** Remove Section 11
- If you don't have insurance, just delete that section
- Not required, but it does build client confidence
